Accommodation

School of English Galway offer our students two differnt types of accommodation. Host family accommodation is for someone who wants to have some experiences of real Irish life by staying with an Irish family. Residence accommodation is for someone who wants to have your own time and meet students from other countries by sharing residence with them.
